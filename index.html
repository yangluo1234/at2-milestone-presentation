Milestone Presentation - Sound Constellation

Conceptual Response

My chosen creative prompt is “No text characters”. The concept of my prototype, Sound Constellation, is to let users create short melodies by connecting stars into constellations. Instead of relying on text, the interface uses visual metaphors such as glowing stars, constellation lines, and common icons(play, reset, reverb) to guide interaction. The aim is to make the interface intuitive, like drawing on the night sky, while sound feedback and random background colour changes add surprise and playfulness. The constellation imagery also reflects the theme of mapping and imagination into sound.
